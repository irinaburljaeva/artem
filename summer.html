Activity 3 · 20 min
Escape Room: Stranded in London 🔐
You've just landed at Heathrow with a dead phone and no cash. Solve 4 puzzles to get to your hotel!
1
The Notice Board
Read carefully — one detail matters
You find a notice board at the airport. It reads:

"Lost & Found: Items must be claimed within 48 hours. The office is located on Level 2, past the currency exchange, next to Gate B. Open Monday–Saturday, 9am–6pm. Items not claimed will be donated to charity."

Your bag went missing. It's Sunday, 8:45am. What should you do first? (Answer in one full sentence — use a modal verb)
2
The Tube Map
Find the odd one out
A stranger gives you a riddle to find a free WiFi hotspot:

"I am a London Underground line. My colour is associated with royalty and luxury. I run through King's Cross, Oxford Circus and Brixton. The other lines are Central, Northern, and Jubilee — but I'm the one you want."

What line is described? (just the name)
3
The Awkward Conversation
Choose the most natural response
You approach a local for directions. They say:

"Sorry mate, you've totally lost me — are you after the tube or d'you want the bus? Either way, you're proper far from Zone 1."

Which response sounds most natural for a B2 English speaker? Type the letter: A, B, or C

A: "I want underground, please."
B: "I'd rather take the tube if it's not too complicated — I'm completely turned around to be honest."
C: "The tube is my preference for transportation to the central zone."
4
The Hotel Voicemail
Fast listening challenge
You find a working phone and hear this voicemail:

"Hi, this is a message for our guest arriving today. Your room won't be ready until 3pm — we do apologise for any inconvenience. In the meantime, feel free to leave your luggage at reception and enjoy our complimentary breakfast until noon. If you need anything, just ask for Sarah."

What TWO things can the guest do while waiting? Answer in your own words.
🎉
You're out! Hotel reached!
All 4 puzzles solved. You'd survive London 🇬🇧
